Frequently Asked Questions

Why did Blockbuster rise in popularity in the 1980s and 1990s?
Blockbuster rose in popularity due to its large selection of movies available for rent, convenient locations, and becoming a household name.
How did Blockbuster maintain its dominance in the video rental industry during this time period?
Blockbuster maintained its dominance by aggressively expanding its store locations, securing exclusive deals with movie studios, and implementing late fees as a revenue stream.
What impact did the rise of Blockbuster have on smaller independent video rental stores?
The rise of Blockbuster led to the decline of many smaller independent video rental stores as they struggled to compete with the larger chains selection and convenience.
What eventually led to the decline of Blockbusters popularity in the early 2000s?
The decline of Blockbuster was fueled by competition from online streaming services like Netflix, changing consumer preferences towards digital media, and poor business decisions made by management.
