For starters, age can have a big impact on the types of genres that appeal to us. Younger viewers might be more drawn to action-packed blockbusters or animated films, while older viewers may prefer dramas or documentaries. And let's not forget about the influence of gender - men and women often have different preferences when it comes to genre.
