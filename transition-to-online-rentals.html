Blockbuster didn't want to change their ways, they thought people would always want to come into the store and browse through the aisles. But they were wrong.

So, businesses need to get with the times and start transitioning to online rentals if they want to stay relevant. It's all about meeting the demands of the consumers, ya know? If companies don't adapt, they'll get left behind in this fast-paced digital world.

But hey, it's not all bad news. Transitioning to online rentals can actually be a good thing for businesses. They can reach a wider audience and attract more customers who prefer the convenience of renting online. It's a win-win situation for everyone involved.

In conclusion, transitioning to online rentals is the way of the future. Businesses need to embrace this change if they want to thrive in today's market. So let's say goodbye to traditional rentals and hello to the wonderful world of online renting!
